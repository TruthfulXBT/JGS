About Block Paving

Derbyshire's Trusted Block Paving Specialists

Block paving became popular around 35–40 years ago, and since then the range of materials, colours, sizes, depths, shapes, textures and patterns has become virtually endless. That's why choosing your new driveway is now a more involved decision than it used to be — and why having an experienced, trusted installer makes all the difference.

At JGS, we use modern block paving with minimal maintenance requirements to achieve that contemporary, polished look that transforms the front of any property. Whether you're in Belper, Ripley, Kilburn, Heanor, Codnor or anywhere across Derbyshire, our team delivers the same exceptional standard on every job.

We are proud members of the Derbyshire County Council Trusted Traders scheme — independently verified reviews from real customers across the county.

  • Full depth excavation and spoil removal
  • MOT Type 1 sub-base preparation and compaction
  • Sand bed laying to correct fall for drainage
  • Block laying in your chosen pattern, colour and texture
  • Edging restraints and kerbing throughout
  • Kiln-dried sand jointing and final compaction
  • Site cleaned and left tidy on completion

Why Choose JGS

No Subcontractors — Ever

Unlike many contractors who quote the job then hand it to someone else, JGS undertake all work ourselves. The team that turns up is the team that does the work — fully trained, fully insured, and committed to the standard our reputation is built on.
