Drainage Work in Derbyshire

Drainage Done Properly — The Foundation of Every Project

When undertaking any patio, driveway or groundworks project, the drainage of surface water is our primary concern. Poor drainage is the most common cause of driveway failure — water that cannot escape will undermine the sub-base over time, causing movement and cracking. At JGS we completely survey the drainage situation before work begins and discuss with you to find the most appropriate, long-lasting solution. Our drainage installations across Belper, Ripley, Heanor, Kilburn and Derbyshire have helped hundreds of homeowners avoid the problems that come with poor-quality installations.

  • Full site drainage survey before works commence
  • Surface water channel and linear drains
  • Soak-away design and installation
  • Connection to existing drainage systems
  • Permeable paving options for SuDS compliance
  • Drainage re-routing for extensions and garages
  • All drainage installed to current regulations
